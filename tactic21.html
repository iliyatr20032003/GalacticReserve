Tactic-21 Rules

Goal: Create a line of three cards that sums exactly to 21.

Setup: Each player starts with the number of random cards selected in the settings, drawn from a deck containing four of each value from 1 to 11.

Turn: On your turn, select a card from your hand and place it on an empty cell. Draw a new card if the deck still has cards.

Winning: You win immediately when you form a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line of your own cards totaling 21. If the board fills without a 21 line, lines totaling 20 are worth 1 point and lines totaling 19 are worth 0.5 points. The higher score wins.
